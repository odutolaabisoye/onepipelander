The Account Limits

₦20k maximum single transfer

You cannot send more than ₦20k out of your account per transaction.

₦50k maximum single deposit

You cannot send more than 50k into your account per transaction.

₦300k maximum balance

The maximum balance that can be in your account is ₦300k. Our partner bank may temporarily block the account if it receives any deposit that makes your balance go over that amount. Not to worry, once you visit a branch and provide the following, your account will be unblocked and upgraded to receive any amount.


  • A utility bill - as proof of address
  • Passport photo - for the bank's records
  • A government recognized ID card - that has both the address and photo above
